The Legacy of Les Paul

Created by Sebastian Garcia, this profile remembers the life and work of Les Paul — a musician and inventor who transformed modern music production.

Who is Les Paul?

Les Paul (1915–2009) was a multi-talented guitarist and songwriter who played American Jazz, country, and blues. He was also one of the most influential inventors in the music industry, pioneering the solid-body electric guitar and multitrack recording.
